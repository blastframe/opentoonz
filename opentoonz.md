- **Animate**: Modifies the position, rotation, and size of the current objects.
- **Selection**: Performs a selection and allows you to transform it.
- **Brush**: Draws in the work area in freehand mode.
- **Geometric**: Draws standard geometric shapes.
- **Type**: Adds text by setting the desired font and size.
- **Fill**: Fills drawing areas with the currently selected style.
- **Paint Brush**: Paints lines and areas specifically in Toonz raster drawings in freehand mode.
- **Eraser**: Deletes vectors in vector drawings, as well as painted areas and lines in raster drawings.
- **Tape**: Joins two open ends of vectors in vector drawings or closes gaps in raster drawings.
- **Style Picker**: Selects a style from the current drawing to make it the active style in the palette and editor.
- **RGB Picker**: Samples red, green, and blue values from the viewer content and applies them to the current style.
- **Control Point Editor**: Modifies a vector's shape by allowing you to edit its underlying control points.
- **Pinch**: Modifies the vector shape by clicking and dragging anywhere directly on the vector line.
- **Pump**: Locally modifies vector thickness by clicking on a section and dragging up or down.
- **Magnet**: Deforms several vectors at once by clicking an area and dragging it.
- **Bender**: Bends and curves vectors in vector drawings.
- **Iron**: Removes creases and flattens vectors by moving the cursor over the desired area.
- **Cutter**: Splits a vector into two separate sections when clicked.
- **Hook**: Defines reference points used in the stage schematic to move an object or link it to another.
- **Tracker**: Tracks specific regions across a sequence of images.
- **Plastic**: Builds a mesh that allows you to seamlessly deform and animate a character or a specific part of it.
- **Zoom**: Zooms in and out of the viewer content by clicking and dragging.
- **Hand**: Pans over the viewer content or moves across the 3D view if activated.
- **Rotate**: Rotates the viewer content or changes your point of view in 3D space.
